Ghost + Docker - The creation of this blog

ghost • Aug 9, 2022


While crafting this awesome website, I aimed to find a solution that blended the simplicity of WordPress with the power to customize features as I pleased. As a Javascript developer, I explored various options and stumbled upon the captivating world of Ghost.

Ghost is a powerful app for new-media creators to publish, share, and grow a business around their content. It comes with modern tools to build a website, publish content, send newsletters & offer paid subscriptions to members.

It is a super simple service to get running, with plenty of themes to download. In addition, because it's written using Javascript and handlebars under the hood, it is easy to extend or modify the functionality.

Ghost comes out of the box with everything you need to create a blog site, complete with a simple WYSIWYG editor, paywalled articles, and marketing emails. A growing community also builds additional plugins that allow easy integration of analytics, comments, and live chat.
